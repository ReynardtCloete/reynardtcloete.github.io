01.  About Me

Hi there! My name is Reynardt Cloete, or just Rey. I love bringing things to life on the internet. I started developing an interest in code around August 2022 after deciding that data analysis wasn't for me. I dabbled in data for about a year prior and still use the skills it afforded me in my own life today.

It is however the creativity of web development that showed me what my passion truly was. I would argue that it's just as fun as building Lego, maybe even more so. It also serves my OCD brain by allowing me to write clean, organised code and build websites that have high attention to detail.

My main area of interest and expertise is front end development (client side of the web) and I'm currently focused on building projects I am passionate about. The goal is to have fun and learn new skills, and potentially land that first job, because what if you could do this all day right!?
